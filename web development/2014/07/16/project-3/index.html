About the Company

The Boston Board Game Company is creating new software technology that revolutionizes the digital board game experience.

The platform is purpose-built to redefine how board games are played on every device.

We are dedicated to helping publishers effectively monetize their existing intellectual property and provide board game enthusiasts new, engaging ways to play their favorite hobby or Euro-style board games.

Innovative Software

We are creating a new digital board game platform to help publishers monetize their intellectual property and create brand new experiences for their customers.

 
 

Our cross-platform software solution allows board game enthusiasts to play anywhere they are - Phone, Tablet, and Laptop. Seamlessly shift from one device to another without friction.

Our software brings board games into the modern digital era. Play when you have a few minutes to spare, or game the entire afternoon.

Candy Crush, Clash of Clans, and Words with Friends allow mobile gamers to play on their own schedule.

Our software translates multi-hour board games into natural mobile experiences while preserving the strategic and intellectual qualities of hobby and Euro-style board gaming.
